Core Features

Everything a Competitive Runner Needs

RunTribe combines real GPS running with strategy, social play, and base building.

Territory Claiming

Every step you take claims ~50m × 50m grid cells on the real map. Your color paints the city.

Capture Rivals

Run through enemy cells to take them over. Every rival territory you claim is a victory for your tribe.

Live GPS Tracking

Real-time distance, pace, elapsed time, and cells claimed — all displayed while you run.

3D Base Building

Construct your headquarters, barracks, watchtower and more. 8 building types, 5 upgrade levels each.

Group Runs

Team up with friends and run together to claim territory collaboratively under your group's flag.

Closed-Loop Capture

Complete a loop while running to instantly claim everything enclosed inside your path.

How It Works

Run. Claim. Dominate.

Three simple steps to own your neighborhood.

01

Start Your Run

Hit the run button on the map screen. Your GPS activates and every cell you pass through gets painted in your color instantly.

02

Capture Enemy Territory

Spot rival-colored cells on the map and run through them. Your color overwrites theirs. Build bigger territory than any runner in your city.

03

Build Your Base

After claiming enough territory, build your headquarters at your most-run location. Upgrade 8 types of buildings across 5 levels to gain strategic advantages.

Social
